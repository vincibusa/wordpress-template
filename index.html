About Us

The Gilded Spoon is a culinary haven where passion meets precision. Founded in 2018 by Chef Isabella Rossi, our restaurant is dedicated to creating unforgettable dining experiences. We blend traditional techniques with modern innovation, using locally sourced ingredients to craft dishes that are both exquisite and sustainable. Our philosophy is simple: to delight our guests with exceptional food, impeccable service, and a warm, inviting atmosphere.
